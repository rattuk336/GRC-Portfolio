# Project 1: Risk Register

## 📌 Project Overview
Built a complete Risk Register for a fictional e-commerce company called **"TechToys"** that sells educational gadgets online.

## 🎯 Objective
Demonstrate the ability to:
- Identify risks across different categories
- Apply likelihood and impact scoring
- Develop mitigation controls
- Calculate inherent vs residual risk

## 🏢 The Company: TechToys
- E-commerce website selling educational toys
- Customer database with credit card information
- 20 employees in a physical office
- Multiple third-party vendors

## 📊 Risk Assessment Methodology

| Component | My Approach |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Likelihood Scale** | 1 (Rare) to 5 (Almost Certain) |
| **Impact Scale** | 1 (Insignificant) to 5 (Catastrophic) |
| **Risk Score** | Likelihood × Impact |
| **Risk Levels** | 1-4 LOW, 5-9 MEDIUM, 10-14 HIGH, 15-25 CRITICAL |

## 📋 Risks Identified

| ID | Risk | Category | Inherent Risk | Residual Risk |
|----|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| R-001 | Data breach (credit card theft) | Cybersecurity | 15 (HIGH) | 10 (MEDIUM) |
| R-002 | Power outage | Operations | 6 (LOW) | 2 (LOW) |
| R-003 | Employee deletes database | Human Error | 20 (HIGH) | 8 (MEDIUM) |
| R-004 | GDPR/compliance violation | Compliance | 15 (HIGH) | 8 (MEDIUM) |
| R-005 | Supplier bankruptcy | Vendor | 8 (MEDIUM) | 3 (LOW) |
| R-006 | Ransomware attack | Cybersecurity | 15 (HIGH) | 8 (MEDIUM) |
| R-007 | Website downtime | Technology | 12 (MEDIUM) | 4 (LOW) |
| R-008 | Physical theft | Physical Security | 8 (MEDIUM) | 3 (LOW) |

## 🔒 Key Controls Implemented
- **Technical:** Firewalls, MFA, encryption, automated backups
- **Administrative:** RBAC, employee training, incident response plan
- **Physical:** Key card access, security cameras, clean desk policy

## 💡 What I Learned

### The Most Important Lesson
A risk register isn't just a list of problems. It's a **decision-making tool** that helps management prioritize where to spend money and effort.

### Why Inherent vs Residual Matters
Before controls: Data breach risk = 15 (CRITICAL) → "We need to fix this NOW"
After controls: Data breach risk = 10 (MEDIUM) → "We can manage this"

This shows the **value of security investments** to management.

### Risk Categories Matter
I initially only thought about cybersecurity risks. But I learned that **operational, compliance, and vendor risks** can be just as damaging.
